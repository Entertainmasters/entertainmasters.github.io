We are Entertainmasters

Entertainmasters often abbreviated as EM is a german movie production company. Located in munich, bavaria and established in 2017 by 2 german friends who loves movie and anime. Mostly knowen in a german school since their whole production team were students in the school and for their Technical & Production Support. We're primarely focusing on movie production & video production

History:

It all started back in 2016 when 2 german students talked about creating movies and animes for living. these students were Copy05 and SamukaiX. They came up with the name Entertainmasters because Copy05 was a proficient at movie production. The team has been formed and only 2 students joined the team. one of the students was DerPilz12000 which their identity keept secret. DerPilz12000's interets was providing soundtracks to Entertainmasters and to animate the animes. Copy05 and SamukaiX decided to partner up with DerPilz12000's already existing buisness Shroomreleace in 2018

The Other student was Gelos he joined as a e-sport athlet for EGM. EGM was formed in 2017 because the Entertainmasters team is formed with Gamers, Content Creators & Coders.

In 2021 DerPilz12000 vanished and broke every contact methods with Entertainmasters that let Entertainmasters acquire Shroomreleace. but not a year later Entertainmasters formed Entertainmasters Musics (EMM / EMMusic)
In 2022 Copy05 decided to become a systemintegrator and software engineer because of his passion for computers and the digital incoming future. he is the everything guy in entertainmasters "the Founder who does everything"
in mid-2023 Entertainmasters acquired ProjectX to revive it and to form ImprovityX a brand that makes motivational videos to train and prepare people for the future.
Originally Entertainmasters was started as a animation studio but has expanded into movie production and IT

Till today Entertainmasters opperates almost digitally and has no office.

Our Vision:

We want to entertain people around the world with movies, streams & videos. we want to create the best content as possible. we also want to connect people who like movies or gaming together. Yes we want people to get together through our or others content.
