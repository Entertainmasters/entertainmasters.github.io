EntertainRewind

EntertainRewind or by it's initialism EMR is a annual new year's eve celebration show established in 2018 hosted by Copy05 to celebrate new years eve and to look back at the year in the Entertainment industry. EntertainRewind features premieres of Entertainmasters projects, Memes & Fails (Central Of Memes), A EDM Music Mix with a countdown and MORE! The Show gets streamed to Youtube at the German Timezone (GMT+1)
